About Us
This, is the journey of VSTravel
The name you trust to explore new worlds and create lasting memories

VSTravel is a leading travel company that has been connecting explorers to the world for 5 years. With a passion for adventure and a commitment to seamless travel experiences, VSTravel ensures that every journey is unforgettable. Whether you seek breathtaking landscapes, vibrant cultures, or hidden gems, we make traveling effortless, exciting, and meaningful.

2020

Where it all started

It all started with a dream. In 2020, a group of passionate travelers, VSTravel was founded with a simple idea: to help people experience the world. Starting as a small community of passionate travelers, we aimed to make travel more accessible and personal.

2021

First Destination Launched

fter a year of research and planning, VSTravel launched its first international travel experience. We partnered with local guides to ensure authentic experiences, making our trips both immersive and memorable.

2022

First Partnership

By 2022, VSTravel had expanded globally, forming partnerships with top travel agencies and hotels to provide seamless journeys. This milestone marked our transition into a well-recognized brand.

2023

All over Indonesia!

Despite global travel restrictions, we found ways to adapt and innovate, ensuring safe and hassle-free travel experiences across Indonesia. Opening more branches in Medan and Surabaya

2024

Group Travel become famous

By 2021, VSTravel was no longer just a travel booking service—it had become a community. With growing demand, we focused on group travel experiences, helping families, friends, and solo travelers connect through exciting journeys.

2025

We are here now, for you

Today, VSTravel stands as a trusted name in the travel industry, having helped thousands of adventurers create lasting memories. Whether you're a solo traveler looking for a remote island escape, a family planning a dream vacation, or a couple seeking a romantic getaway, we’re here to make it happen.
